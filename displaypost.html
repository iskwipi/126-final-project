Adobong Manok

Sir Nikko

(50 ratings)

Adobong Manok is a Filipino chicken stew made with soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, and peppercorns. It is a popular dish in the Philippines and is often served with rice.

Tags:

#adobo #chicken #stew

Servings

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg chicken
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1 garlic
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp peppercorns
  • Pinch salt
